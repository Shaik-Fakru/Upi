Secure Your UPI Transactions

AI-powered fraud detection for digital payments

With the rapid adoption of UPI for digital transactions, the risk of fraud has increased. Our system uses a Random Forest classifier to analyze transaction details like bank name, transaction ID, and amount. It classifies transactions as "Failed: Incorrect Details" or "Successful: Verified," enhancing security and ensuring smooth processing of legitimate transactions.
